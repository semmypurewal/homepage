About Me

Hi there! I'm Semmy Purewal, an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at UNC Asheville. I'm interested in Social Media, Web Application Programming, and Tech-related Community Activism. In 2005, I co-founded Free IT Athens, an all-volunteer non-profit organization in Athens, GA, which is still going strong nearly 7 years later.
